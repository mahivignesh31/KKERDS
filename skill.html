DDU-GKY & AP MEPMA Programs

Empowering Youth & Communities

Unlock Your Potential with Our Skill Development Initiatives

KK Educational and Rural Development Society is dedicated to transforming lives through skill development. We are proud to implement government-backed programs like Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) and initiatives linked with MEMA (Mission for Elimination of Poverty in Municipal Areas).

DDU-GKY: This flagship program aims to skill rural youth and provide them with jobs having regular monthly wages or above the minimum wages. It focuses on sustainable employment and is a crucial part of the National Rural Livelihood Mission (NRLM).

AP MEPMA-linked Programs: Our courses aligned with MEMA (or similar urban poverty alleviation missions) target the urban poor, providing them with vocational training to enhance their employability and income-generating capacities, fostering self-reliance and social upliftment.

About Us

Welcome to KK Educational and Rural Development Society

Founded with a vision to bridge the urban-rural divide, KK Educational and Rural Development Society is dedicated to transforming lives through quality education, targeted skill development, and holistic community empowerment.

Through our flagship training centers like KK Computers, we deliver government-recognized courses under prestigious schemes like DDU-GKY, PMKVY, and APSSDC. Our curriculum is meticulously designed to be practical, job-oriented, and aligned with industry demands, ensuring every learner becomes confident, capable, and career-ready.
